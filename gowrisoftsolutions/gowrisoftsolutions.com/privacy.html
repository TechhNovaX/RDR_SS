Privacy Policy

Gowri Software Solutions respects the privacy and security of its users and subscribers. In order to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy, we have prepared this statement disclosing the privacy practices for the entire Website. By accessing or using this Website, you agree to Gowri’s Privacy Policy, as outlined below. Gowri Software Solutions reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy from time to time at its sole discretion. Your use of this Website will be subject to the most current version of the Privacy Policy at the time of such use.

The below sets out the policy of Gowri Software Solutions Limited as to the information which is gathered and distributed through its Website.

With respect to information collected:

For each user or subscriber to the Website, our server does not recognize any information regarding your domain or e-mail address. Sessions of each user or subscriber to the website are monitored, but each user or subscriber remains anonymous.

With respect to the type of information that the website collects:

The Website collects only that information which is volunteered by each user or subscriber, such as survey information and/or site registrations and the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail.

With respect to use of the information collected:

The very limited information collected is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes and is used solely for the benefit of each user or subscriber in the following manner:

  • for internal review, after which the information is discarded;
  • to improve the content of the Website;
  • used to notify each user or subscriber about updates to the Website.

With respect to email addresses:

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail. Information is volunteered by each user or subscriber, such as survey information, site registrations, comments and/or questions from each user or subscriber. Such information is collected via direct submission from the individual user or subscriber. We do not send e-mails as a form of solicitation. We may however, send you an email in response to any questions and/or comments you might have about our website. Although your e-mail address is kept on file within our database, we will never use it to send you follow-up e-mails, or solicit you in anyway.

With respect to links:

Gowri Software Solutions’s Website contains links to external websites in order to better facilitate your visit or provide with requisite information you require. However, Gowri Software Solutions is not responsible for the content or the privacy practices employed by third party websites. This Privacy statement does not apply to any websites that are linked from this website.

With respect to cookies:

Use of cookies:

Cookies are small text files that, like most other Web servers, we place in your device that you use to access our website. This is done to recognize your device during a session or in your future visits to our website, primarily in order to provide better user experience.

Types of cookies used:

Session cookie: Session cookies remain only as long as a browsing session is active, and are intended to avoid user inconvenience during browsing. These cookies allow websites to link the actions of a user during a browser session and expire at the end of the browsing session. Session cookies also assist the user in navigating the website, and allow the user to access secure parts of the webpage when logged in.

With respect to security:

Gowri Software Solutions has appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our Web site.
